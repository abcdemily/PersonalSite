Emily Meltzer

graphic designer, photographer, web designer, typography nerd. and lover of all things design related!

If you were a typeface, what would you be?

I was asked this question once on a job interview, and it was my most favorite question! I would be Bodoni. It is a beautiful and classic font. I love how each variant has something special about it. The Bodoni Italic ampersand is my favorite. (I could spend hours drawing ampersands).

What helps you get motivated when you are having a creative “brain fart”?

I could say I take the Michael Scott approach and have someone spin me around in my chair because “a spinning brain is a working brain”, but i’ll leave that technique to him.

I like to start by plugging in my headphones. Next to design, music has always been a huge part of my life. I love how it inspires me and gets my creative juices flowing. Next, I will browse my favorite creative sites. The Dieline for packaging, Logopond for logos, Designspiration for anything, and of course Pinterest. I also love Reddit. You never know when a creative strike is going to happen!
